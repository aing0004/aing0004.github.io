Photography

Genesis Series

A photography-based project that explores the combination of photographs and illustration, as well as the idea of coexistence between humankind and nature.

8-9 pm

A photography series based on documenting sunsets and cloud formations on various days during the summer months of 2019. I became deeply intrigued by the transition of day into night, sunlight into darkness. I wanted to document the unique visual atmospheric changes that occur during this ordinary event/liminal space. Almost every photo was shot between 8pm and 9pm. The photos were later digitally overlaid to create surreal visuals.
